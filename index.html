The Secret Life of Pets

Character Description Role
Max is a jack russel terrier and the main character of the story. He very much loves his owner Katie and has a strong emotional attachment to her. Max often refuses to share anything with Duke, another pet. He becomes possesive of Katie whenever Duke comes along. Protagonist
Norman is a fat guinea pig and in the movie, he is known for always getting lost and trying to find his apartment. He is the protagonist in the mini movie, "Norman Television". Protagonist
Gidget is a white pomeranian with big blue eyes and a pink bow on her head, she is one of the main characters. She is very jolly and talkative, despite her small size, she can be very brave and incredibly tough Protagonist
Duke is a large brown mutt. He has long and shaggy brown fur. After Max got adopted, Duke did not have a good relationship with him and usually stole his food but later on they became good friends. Protagonist
Snowball is an adorable cute white rabbit however, he hates the petowners and the pets. He formed an army for abandoned pets and wants to take revenge. Antagonist
